Badges and beyond - Linkedin and other credentialling ideas

As regular readers would know, P2PU was an early and active developer of a badges platform, fostering an ecosystem for micro-credentialling that remains in use to this day. Finding fast and flexible ways to report mastery of skills that matter remains a challenging problem. 

We are always chuffed, then, to see learners finding new ways of reporting their achievements - for example as educational milestones in their Linkedin profiles. 

This is a great example of how online communities find new ways to report competencies. Flexible frameworks, portfolios of achievement, peer-endorsements...there are so many valid alternatives to the traditional college transcript.

Have you seen any interesting new approaches to credentialling online? Join the converastion on our forums <link>, or see more of our musings on badges and on assessment generally.
