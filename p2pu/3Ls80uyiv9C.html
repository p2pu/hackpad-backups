Licences 

  • When you remix content (take existing content, but not adapt/modify) you can’t apply a new license
  • The original underlying work stays licensed under original license
  • In practice:
    • Use a CC BY licensed picture to include in a P2PU blog post
    • P2PU blog posts are CC BY SA licensed
    • The picture requires attribution and a note that the original image is CC BY licensed
